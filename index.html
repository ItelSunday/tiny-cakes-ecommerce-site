Our Story

The Tiny Cakes Bakery business initially opened in Los Angeles in April 2015. Soon after, they generated attention when they won Los Angeles New Times Best Bakery, The best-kept secret in Los Angeles turned into a regional favorite. With a location in South California Sweetness Bakeshop hits the spot with their creative combination of flavors, some of which are so unique, they've become synonymous with Los Angeles. Their signature desserts, including the Guava and Cheese Cupcakes, Cookies n' Cream, Black Cookies and Key Lime Dessert Shots support an ever-changing menu that also includes limited-edition and seasonal flavors. Sweetness has been acclaimed by Food and Wine Magazine as having one of the Nation's Best New Cupcakes, Ramsey ranked them one of the top 10 Donut in the Country and Los Angeles New Times named them Los Angeles's Best Bakery and Los Angeles's Best Cupcakes.
